Sadabai Raisoni Womens' College is an Autonomous Institution affiliated to SNDT Womens' University, Mumbai nestled in the city of Nagpur. The Institute has been accredited with NAAC A grade. Under the umbrella of the renowned Raisoni Group of Institutions, the Institute continues to be a significant portrayal of the thought process which bespeaks ‘Education for all’. This Institute provides Education for Girls only.

Courses Available :

Three UG courses for Science and Technology :
1. B.Sc.-IT (Bachelor of Science Information and Technology)
2. BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application)
3. B.Sc.-FND (Bachelor of Science in Food Nutrition)

Two PG courses :
1. MSc-CS (Master of Science Computer Science)
2. MSc-FSN (Master of Science Food Nutrition)
